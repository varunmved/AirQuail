How does airQuail enable smart cities and smart homes?

airQuail monitors pollutant levels, temperature and humidity conditions. Smart sensors log data onto the cloud which generates analytics based on data captured to derive results such as air quality index and weather conditions in localized regions. Smart-city and smart-home applications can leverage this crowd-sourced data to drive decisions on environment, infrastructure and lifestyle!
